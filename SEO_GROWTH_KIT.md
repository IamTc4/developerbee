## 7. Internal Linking Plan

### Strategy
1.  **Hub and Spoke:** Use the Blog (Spoke) to link to Services (Hub).
    *   *Example:* A blog on "E-commerce Automation" links to the "Automation Services" page.
2.  **Cross-Linking Projects:** Projects should link to the specific Service used.
    *   *Example:* A "FinTech App" project page links to "Mobile App Development" service.
3.  **Contextual Footer:** Ensure high-value pages (Services, Pricing, Portfolio) are in the footer of every page.
4.  **Breadcrumbs:** Implement breadcrumbs on deep pages (Blogs, Projects) to link back to parent categories.

### Implementation Checklist
- [ ] Blog posts link to at least 1 related Service page.
- [ ] Service pages link to 2-3 relevant case studies (Projects).
- [ ] Homepage links to latest 3 Blogs.
- [ ] "Related Projects" section at the bottom of every Project page.

### Month 3: Outreach & Guest Posting
21. **Guest Post on Tech Blogs:** Reach out to mid-tier tech blogs.
22. **Podcast Appearances:** Pitch founders as guests on tech/business podcasts.
23. **Local Directories:** YellowPages, Yelp (if applicable).
24. **Startup Directories:** BetaList, ProductHunt (for new tool launches).
25. **Partner Links:** Ask technology partners for a link exchange.
26. **Testimonial Link Building:** Give testimonials to tools you use in exchange for a link.
27. **Broken Link Building:** Find broken tech links on other sites and offer your blog as replacement.

29. **Infographic Submission:** Submit infographics to visual directories.
30. **University Job Boards:** Post "internships" or "jobs" (often high DA .edu links).
