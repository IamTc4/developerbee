Healthcare Portal
UI/UX Design • 3 months
← Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

A patient management system with an intuitive interface for appointments, records, and telemedicine. Designed for accessibility and ease of use for all ages.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Designing a **HIPAA-compliant** interface and workflow.
  • Ensuring high **Accessibility (WCAG)** for elderly and visually impaired users.
  • Integrating a seamless and secure **telemedicine** platform.
  • Implementing robust **Multi-language support** across all static content.

Technologies Used

  • Figma
  • Adobe XD
  • Zeplin
  • React.js
  • Tailwind CSS

Results & Impact

  • Patient Satisfaction: 4.9/5 (Achieved high user feedback score)
  • Appointment Booking: +120% (Significant increase in online bookings)
  • Support Calls: -40% (Reduction in help requests due to improved UX)
  • Accessibility: AAA (Met highest level of WCAG compliance)
